# Kimia Protocol: Automated Yield Optimization on Solana

## What is Kimia Protocol?

Kimia Protocol is a yield optimization platform built on Solana that automatically allocates user funds across the highest-yielding DeFi opportunities. Think of it as an intelligent yield aggregator that continuously monitors APY rates across lending protocols, liquidity pools, and staking platforms to maximize returns.

## How Kimia Works

### Automated Strategy Execution
1. Users deposit assets (USDC, SOL, etc.) into Kimia vaults
2. The protocol's smart contracts scan available yield sources in real-time
3. Funds are automatically rebalanced to the highest-yielding strategy
4. Returns are compounded automatically

### Supported Yield Sources
- **Lending:** Solend, MarginFi, Drift
- **Liquidity Pools:** Orca, Raydium, Meteora
- **Staking:** Marinade (mSOL), JitoSOL, bSOL
- **Structured Products:** Custom strategies unique to Kimia

### Risk Management
- Diversified exposure across protocols
- Smart contract audits for all integrated protocols
- Emergency withdrawal mechanisms
- Configurable risk tiers (conservative/balanced/aggressive)
