# Trepa: Precision Prediction Markets on Solana

## What is Trepa?

Trepa is a next-generation prediction market platform built on the Solana blockchain that fundamentally changes how users engage with forecasting contests. Unlike traditional binary prediction markets that only reward correct directional bets, Trepa ties payouts directly to **numerical accuracy** — the closer your prediction is to the actual resolved price, the bigger your reward.

## How Trepa Works

### The 60-Second Round

Each round on Trepa lasts exactly **one minute**, split into two phases:

1. **Prediction Phase (30 seconds):** Users submit their forecast — typically Bitcoin's price at settlement.
2. **Resolution & Settlement Phase (30 seconds):** The oracle resolves the actual price, payouts are calculated based on proximity, and winners receive USDC instantly.

### Precision-Based Rewards

- **Binary markets:** Win or lose based on direction only.
- **Trepa:** The closer your prediction, the larger your share of the prize pool.

This rewards genuine analytical skill. A user predicting BTC at $104,230 when the actual is $104,250 earns far more than someone who predicted $105,000 — even if both were directionally correct.

### Flash Pools

Trepa introduces **Flash Pools** — rapid-entry pools with low minimum stakes for high-frequency traders:
- Small entry amounts with asymmetric upside
- Instant settlement and payout in USDC
- Leaderboard ranking based on cumulative accuracy

## Why Solana?

- **Sub-second finality:** 30-second settlement requires millisecond confirmation times
- **Near-zero fees:** Participate in many rounds without gas costs eating into rewards
- **Native USDC:** Seamless stablecoin payouts
- **High throughput:** Thousands of concurrent predictions without congestion
